About Us

Changing the way we view healthcare, one biomarker at a time.

Sudore was founded because our founders saw the increasing need for proactive-rather than reactive-healthcare.

Our founders recognized that the current "sick-care" system was not optimized to prevent disease, rather to treat it once the damage had been done. They then created Sudore, a company dedicated to leading the charge in proactive healthcare, which like its name implies, wanted to harness the largely untapped power of novel biomarkers in sweat to monitor for diseases. At Sudore, we believe that the future of healthcare lies in proactivity and we're here to make that future a reality.
